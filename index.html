Otis P. Wilbursnoot


2005 - 2017


Puppy-at-Large

Mr. Wilbursnoot, affectionally known as Oatmeal, Odie, Otis, or Oats to his family, entered the heart and home of his owner, Brooke. He was adopted to be a loyal companion to Molly the Shih-tzu. Ultimately, he became not only her best buddy but also a friend to every person and canine he met. His days were filled with squirrels, walks, peanut butter, and basking lazily in the sun.

The Early Years

Oatmeal hailed from Canon City, Colorado under the given name Kermit. Although his natural parents and siblings are unknown, his history shows he was a puppy-at-large. He was but a lonely pup on city streets, living a life of straydom. The day came when the local animal control took Oatmeal into custody, booked him into a dog pound, which, partnered with the Cotopaxi Poodle Preserve, posted his picture online. This picture captured Odie's happy nature and sweet temperatment, which ultimately captured my heart. Within a week, his adoption fee was paid and he was joyfully running through the yard with Shih-tzu Molly and me.
